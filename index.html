About Us

We are Hakim Ghanoudi and Mathieu De Keyser, 3rd-year IT & Business students at AP Hogeschool. This project was developed as part of our Business Intelligence coursework. The primary focus was on data cleansing, transformation, and ETL processes to create an insightful Power BI dashboard for World Wide Importers.

The website serves as an additional layer to present the project and provide an intuitive interface for exploring the dashboard. We combined our skills in database management, data analysis, and web development to deliver a complete solution tailored to meet business requirements.
